# Community standards

This project exists to make game-development knowledge more useful to everyone. Participation should reflect that purpose.

Be respectful, specific, and curious. Critique claims and evidence rather than people. Make room for different disciplines, levels of experience, genres, cultures, accessibility needs, and development philosophies. Disagreement is welcome when it clarifies conditions and tradeoffs.

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, personal attacks, deliberate disruption, and publishing another person’s private information are not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when necessary to protect a constructive community. If a discussion becomes harmful, contact the maintainer through the repository rather than escalating it publicly.
